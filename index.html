ChristmasColouring

Source code: ChristmasColouring

Built with Processing and Processing.js

Instructions

Paint onto the background image with the mouse.

Move sliders around the image to change the Hue (H), Saturation (S), Brightness (B), and Opacity (O) of the paint.

The inner border shows the current paint colour.

Press '+' to increase brush size, '-' to decrease brush size, 'c' to clear, 's' to save an image
